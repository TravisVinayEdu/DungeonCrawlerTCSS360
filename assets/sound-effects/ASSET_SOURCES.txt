Sound effects used by Dungeon Crawler
=====================================

These effects are derived from audio created by Kenney and released under
Creative Commons Zero (CC0). Attribution is not required.

Kenney Interface Sounds:
https://kenney.nl/assets/interface-sounds

Files selected from the WAV distribution hosted for the Godot Asset Library:
https://github.com/Calinou/kenney-interface-sounds

menu-button.wav  <- click_001.wav
pillar-found.wav <- confirmation_001.wav
battle-win.wav   <- confirmation_004.wav
battle-lose.wav  <- error_002.wav

Kenney RPG Audio:
https://kenney.nl/assets/rpg-audio

The CC0 RPG pack was downloaded through Kenney's OpenGameArt release:
https://opengameart.org/content/50-rpg-sound-effects

attack.wav   <- knifeSlice.ogg, converted to WAV for Java Sound playback
